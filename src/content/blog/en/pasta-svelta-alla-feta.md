Feta never lets you down!

## Quick info

- **Servings:** 2 people
- **Prep:** 5 minutes
- **Cooking:** 10 minutes
- **Tools:** air fryer, pasta pot, tray or basket that fits in the air fryer
- **Heads-up:** always keep a bit of pasta water aside to bring the sauce together when you toss

## Ingredients

- 1 block of feta (about half a block per person)
- fresh cherry tomatoes to taste
- extra virgin olive oil
- salt to taste
- dried oregano to taste
- 160g (5.6 oz) pasta (for 2)

## Method

1. **Prep the sauce.** Place the feta and the washed, halved cherry tomatoes in the air-fryer tray. Season with oil, salt and oregano.
2. **Cook.** Cook in the air fryer at 190°C (374°F) for 10 minutes, until the feta is soft and the cherry tomatoes have collapsed.
3. **Cook the pasta.** Meanwhile, cook the pasta in plenty of salted water. Reserve a bit of the cooking water.
4. **Toss it together.** Tip the drained pasta into the tray with the feta and cherry tomatoes. Stir until you get a creamy sauce, splashing in a little pasta water if needed.
5. **Serve.** Finish with a drizzle of raw oil and a pinch of oregano (or chilli, if that's your thing).
