Feta lovers, when you don't have time but want a yummy first course, slap it in the oven and go!

## Quick info

- **Portions:** 2 people
- **Preparation:** 45 minutes (oven + dough in parallel)
- **Baking:** 25-30 minutes
- **Tools:** baking dish, pasta pot, fork, knife, chopping board

## Ingredients

- 150g (5.3 oz) pasta (size as desired)
- 100g (3.5 oz) feta cheese
- 250g (8.8 oz) cherry tomatoes (datterini or cherry)
- Chopped fresh basil or oregano to taste
- Extra virgin olive oil to taste
- Salt to taste

## Procedure

1. **Prepare the cherry tomatoes:** Wash the 250g (8.8 oz) cherry tomatoes. If they are large, cut them in half lengthwise.
2. **Profile:** Grease the bottom with a little oil. Mix the cherry tomatoes with 2 tablespoons of oil, a pinch of salt and the chosen spice, then place them in the baking dish from the cut side.
3. **Feta:** rinse the feta from the brine and place it in the centre of the baking dish, between the cherry tomatoes. Add a drizzle of oil and a pinch of spice.
4. **Bake in a static oven at 200°C (392°F) preheated for **25-30 minutes**. The cherry tomatoes and feta should be soft and juicy, not dry.
5. **Cook the pasta:** in the meantime cook the pasta in salted water and drain when al dente, keeping some of the cooking water aside.
6. **Condimento:** take the dish out of the oven and mash the feta and cherry tomatoes with a fork until you have a creamy sauce.
7. **Mantecing:** add the pasta to the sauce and toss, adding 1-2 tablespoons of cooking water if needed. Stir well and serve immediately.
